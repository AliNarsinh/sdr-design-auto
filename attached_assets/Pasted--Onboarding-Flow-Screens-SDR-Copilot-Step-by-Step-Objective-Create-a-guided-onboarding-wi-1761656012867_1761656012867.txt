# Onboarding Flow Screens — SDR Copilot (Step‑by‑Step)

**Objective:** Create a guided onboarding wizard that collects project context, connects data sources, defines one or more ICPs, ingests an initial lead list, and then automatically prioritizes leads into *Researching* vs *Ready to Push* in **Your Leads**.

---

## Flow Overview (Stepper)

1. **Welcome & Project Details**
2. **Company URLs & Data Sources**
3. **ICPs (Single or Multiple)**
4. **Upload Initial ICP Lead List**
5. **Review & Start**
6. **Post‑Onboarding Transition → Your Leads (Prioritized)**

Each step shows: title, short description, progress (e.g., 1/5), Back/Next buttons, and Save & Exit.

---

## Step 1 — Welcome & Project Details

**Goal:** Capture the project’s purpose and context.

**Fields:**

* *Project Name* (text)
* *What does your company do?* (textarea, 2–4 sentences)
* *Primary Value Proposition / Outcomes* (chips or textarea)
* *Primary Geography* (multi‑select)
* *Target Personas* (multi‑select; e.g., VP Sales, RevOps, CTO)
* *Compliance Preferences* (checkboxes: approve‑before‑first‑send, opt‑out policy, LinkedIn manual only)

**UX Notes:**

* Right panel with live preview card *“This is how we’ll present you in emails”*.
* Validation: require *Project Name* and *What does your company do?*

**Edge Cases:**

* If no personas selected, allow free‑text persona notes.

---

## Step 2 — Company URLs & Data Sources

**Goal:** Provide official sources for research; optionally connect document folders.

**Fields:**

* *Primary Website URL* (required)
* *Key URLs* (repeatable): About, Product, Pricing, Blog, Careers, Press/News, Docs/Integrations
* *Document Sources* (choose any):

  * Upload Folder (drag‑and‑drop)
  * Cloud Drive link (paste URL)
  * Knowledge base link
* *Third‑Party Signals* (optional toggles): Funding DB, Jobs feed, Website intent provider

**Behavior:**

* Show status badges: Connected / Pending / Skipped.
* Warn if Pricing or Careers URL missing (helps intent/hiring signals).

**Edge Cases:**

* For external links, validate reachable domain; allow skip with reason.

---

## Step 3 — ICPs (Single or Multiple)

**Goal:** Define one or more ICPs the system will score against.

**Layout:** Tabs or vertically stacked *ICP Cards* with **Add ICP** button.

**Per ICP Fields:**

* *ICP Name* (e.g., “Mid‑Market SaaS US”, “EMEA Fintech 200–1000”)
* *Industries* (multi‑select)
* *Company Size Bands* (checkboxes: 1–50, 51–200, 201–1000, 1000+)
* *Geographies* (multi‑select)
* *Tech Stack Signals* (checkboxes: CRM: HubSpot/SFDC; Data: Snowflake; etc.)
* *Trigger Signals* (chips): hiring keywords, funding stages, product launches
* *Disqualifiers* (chips): agencies, competitors, student orgs, free‑only

**Sidebar:** *ICP Scoring Formula Preview*

* +30 industry match, +20 size band, +15 geo, +10 CRM presence, +10 trigger signal last 60 days, −15 low activity

**Bulk Actions:** Duplicate ICP, Archive ICP.

**Edge Cases:**

* Require: ICP Name + at least 1 industry + 1 size band + 1 geo.

---

## Step 4 — Upload Initial ICP Lead List

**Goal:** Seed the system with target accounts/contacts that match the ICPs.

**Inputs:**

* *Upload CSV* or *Paste Table* (Company, Domain, Contact Name, Title, Email, LinkedIn, Phone, Assigned ICP)
* *Map Columns* step with auto‑detect; remember mapping for future uploads
* *Assign ICP* (dropdown per row if not provided)
* *Assign Sequence* (optional now, or later in Sequences)

**Validation & Preview:**

* Show counts: New Companies / Updated / Duplicates / Invalids
* Highlight rows missing required fields (Company or Domain)

**Post‑Upload Processing (background):**

* Status badges per row: **Importing → Enriching → Researching → Ready to Push**
* If contact emails bounce on validation, mark *Needs Fix* and exclude from Ready list

**Edge Cases:**

* Duplicate detection by domain + contact email; offer merge.

---

## Step 5 — Review & Start

**Goal:** Confirm setup and explain what happens next.

**Summary Cards:**

* Project Overview (from Step 1)
* Data Sources connected (from Step 2)
* ICPs defined (list with counts)
* Leads uploaded (numbers per ICP)
* Default Sequence selection (optional)

**Start Button:** **Start Prioritizing Leads**

* On click: route to **Your Leads** with priority sort.

**Helper Copy:**

* “We’ll prioritize leads and split them into *Researching* and *Ready to Push*. You can work from the *To‑Do* view immediately.”

---

## Post‑Onboarding — Your Leads Prioritization

**Goal:** Ensure the list is usable with hundreds of leads; prioritize by readiness.

**New Lead Statuses:**

* **Importing** → just added
* **Enriching** → filling gaps (domain, industry, role)
* **Researching** → generating brief + signal hooks
* **Ready to Push** → first email/LI/call prepared, awaiting review
* **In Sequence** → scheduled/sending

**Default Sort:**

1. *Ready to Push* (soonest next action)
2. *Researching* (closest to completion)
3. *Needs Fix* (missing data/bounce)

**Filters:** Status, ICP, Owner, Intent band, Sequence.

**Batch Actions:** Approve & Send for selected *Ready to Push*; Move to Nurture; Reassign ICP.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

1. Wizard enforces step completion; users can Save & Exit and resume at the last step.
2. Multiple ICPs can be created/edited; scoring preview updates live.
3. Upload accepts CSV/paste; column mapping is remembered; duplicates handled.
4. After finishing, **Page 1** disappears; ICP/data edits move to Dashboard > Settings.
5. **Your Leads** shows statuses with the new pipeline (*Importing → Enriching → Researching → Ready to Push → In Sequence*).
6. Default sort surfaces *Ready to Push* first; list remains responsive with **500+ leads**.

"Design Step 4: ‘Upload Leads’. Provide tabs for Upload CSV and Paste Table. After upload, show a Column Mapping step with auto‑detect and editable selectors. Show a preview table with row status badges: Importing, Enriching, Researching, Ready to Push, Needs Fix. Provide Assign ICP dropdown and optional Assign Sequence. Show counts for New/Updated/Duplicates/Invalids. Progress (4/5), Back/Next/Save & Exit."

"Design Step 5: ‘Review & Start’. Show summary cards for Project Overview, Data Sources, ICPs, Leads uploaded, Default Sequence. CTA button: ‘Start Prioritizing Leads’. Include helper text explaining that Your Leads will be sorted into Researching vs Ready to Push. Progress (5/5), Back, Finish, Save & Exit."

"Design the Your Leads page that appears immediately after onboarding. Provide a status filter bar and default sort showing Ready to Push first, then Researching, then Needs Fix. Each row displays Company, Contact, ICP, Status, Next Action, Due. Include batch actions (Approve & Send, Move to Nurture, Reassign ICP). Clicking a row opens the detailed lead view with Info, Tasks, and Chat."
